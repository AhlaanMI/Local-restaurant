Our story

A neighborhood table built by family

Cedar Grove Kitchen is family-owned, wood-fired, and proudly Austin. We cook with Texas farms, make our sauces in-house, and keep the music warm so you can settle in.

From our kitchen

House-made pastas, slow-braised meats, and wood-fired doughs anchor the menu. We tweak dishes with the seasons to keep flavors bright and honest.

How we host

Warm lighting, attentive service, and a soundtrack that keeps the room buzzing. Whether it is date night, family dinner, or cocktails with friends, we have a table ready.
